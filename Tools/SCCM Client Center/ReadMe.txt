SCCM Client Center (SMSCliCtr)
Copyright (c) 2009 by Roger Zander

1)Summary:
The tool is designed for IT Professionals to troubleshoot SMS/SCCM Client related Issues. 
The SCCM Client Center provides a quick and easy overview of client settings, 
including running services and SCCM settings in a good easy to use, user interface.
